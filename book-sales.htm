Our Friends’ Book Nook continues to hold sales throughout the year. Look for it in the right hand corner as you enter the Library.

While browsing, pick up a copy of the Book Page, a publication Friends’ funds provide our readers.


Book Sales

Two of the ways the Friends support the Bonita Springs Library are through book sales in the Library and through ongoing book sales within the library building. Every dollar raised during the sales goes directly back to your library.

Sales are held two times a year. Among the items represented are fiction and non-fiction, cookbooks, bestsellers, children's books, puzzles, audio CDs, and DVDs.

Check the Calendar for current dates!

Volunteers also assist at the book sales. lf you would like more information about volunteering, please send us an email.
