We're Going Green

The Friends of the Library Board is trying to be as green as possible. In order to accomplish this we need to use e-mail as much as possible.

If you have not already done so, please send us your e-mail address. This will only be used for Friends business and will not be given or sold to any other group.

Just send your address to bonitaspringsfriends@gmail.com


Join

The best way to support your library is to become a member. Memberships are valid from January 1 - December 31.

  • Receive periodic internet broadcast messages about current happenings
  • Pre-view access to our annual book sales held in the spring and fall
  • Receive a bi-annual newsletter
  • Meet new Friends and use your talents to give back to the community
  • Volunteer opportunities await you
