How to Help

Donate

The Friends of the Bonita Springs Public Library, Inc. is a non-profit organization in Florida. Donations are tax deductible as allowed by law.

What we Accept

  • Books in Good Condition
  • Audio CDs
  • CDs
  • DVDs in Good Condition
  • We also accept regular sized Readers’ Digest books in Good Condition

Monetary Gifts

The Friends gratefully accept monetary donations to provide funding that might not otherwise be met by the Library’s operating budget. Friends of the Library is a Florida 501(c)3 corporation. As such, your donations are tax deductible, subject to tax law.

We Do Not Accept

  • Encyclopedias
  • Text Books
  • Magazines
  • Damaged, damp or moldy books
  • Home recorded/burned CDs or DVD’s
  • Loose disks without their original cases/packaging
